## Responsibilities

### QA/QC Management

As Site Coordinator, I was responsible for comprehensive quality assurance and control throughout the construction phase:

- **British Standards Compliance**: Ensured all construction activities met rigorous British Standards requirements specified by Mandarin Oriental brand
- **Quality Inspections**: Conducted systematic inspections of all construction phases, from foundations to final finishes
- **Materials Approval**: Reviewed and approved all materials, finishes, and FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment) selections
- **Mock-up Reviews**: Supervised creation and approval of mock-ups for critical elements (bathrooms, bedrooms, facades)

### Site Coordination

- **Main Contractor Management**: Coordinated daily activities of the main contractor and multiple sub-contractors
- **International PM Liaison**: Worked closely with **Mace Group (UK)**, one of the world's leading project management consultancies
- **Schedule Monitoring**: Tracked construction progress against project milestones and critical path activities
- **Site Meetings**: Chaired weekly progress meetings with contractors, consultants, and design teams

### Client Representative Services

- **Investor Reporting**: Prepared comprehensive monthly progress reports for international investors
- **Design Coordination**: Liaised between design offices, brand representatives, and construction teams
- **Change Management**: Managed design changes, variations, and their impact on schedule and budget
- **Document Control**: Maintained comprehensive project documentation using systematic filing and tracking systems
