## Responsibilities

### QA/QC Management

As QA/QC Manager for this flagship project, my responsibilities included:

- **British Standards Compliance**: Ensured all construction activities strictly adhered to British Standards, as required for 7-star standard developments
- **Quality Plans & Procedures**: Developed and implemented comprehensive quality management systems
- **Materials Approval**: Reviewed and approved all premium materials, finishes, and imported products
- **Testing & Commissioning**: Supervised extensive testing protocols for all building systems
- **Inspection Protocols**: Conducted systematic inspections at every construction phase
- **Defect Management**: Implemented rigorous snagging and defect rectification processes
- **Documentation Control**: Maintained comprehensive project documentation using Oracle Aconex

### Client Representative Services

- **Investor Reporting**: Prepared detailed monthly progress reports for high-net-worth private client
- **Design Coordination**: Liaised between London-based design offices (Purcell, Lambert & Browne) and local contractors
- **International Communication**: Daily coordination with UK-based consultants in English
- **Variation Management**: Managed design changes and their impact on schedule and budget
- **Budget Monitoring**: Tracked project costs and expenditures against approved budgets

### Construction Supervision

- **Main Contractor Management**: Supervised and coordinated main contractor and numerous specialized sub-contractors
- **Progress Monitoring**: Tracked construction progress against approved schedules using Primavera P6
- **Technical Problem Solving**: Provided engineering solutions for complex construction challenges
- **Safety Oversight**: Ensured compliance with international health and safety standards
- **Site Coordination**: Managed daily site activities and contractor interfaces
