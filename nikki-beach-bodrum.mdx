## Responsibilities

### QA/QC Management

As Site Coordinator, I ensured every aspect of construction met Nikki Beach's prestigious brand standards:

- **Brand Compliance**: Implemented quality control procedures aligned with Nikki Beach global standards
- **Materials Selection**: Approved premium finishes, furniture, and decorative elements meeting brand specifications
- **Workmanship Standards**: Supervised construction quality across all trades (structural, MEP, finishes, landscaping)
- **Mock-up Approvals**: Coordinated creation and approval of sample rooms, bathrooms, and public spaces
- **Defect Management**: Systematic snagging and punch-list completion before handover

### Site Coordination & Supervision

- **Daily Site Inspections**: Conducted comprehensive quality inspections across all construction zones
- **Contractor Management**: Coordinated main contractor and specialized sub-contractors (MEP, finishes, FF&E)
- **Schedule Monitoring**: Tracked progress against project milestones and opening deadlines
- **Safety Compliance**: Ensured all construction activities met health and safety regulations
- **Technical Problem Solving**: Provided engineering solutions for site challenges

### Client Representative Services

- **Investor Communication**: Regular progress reporting to project stakeholders
- **Brand Liaison**: Coordinated with Nikki Beach brand representatives for design and operational requirements
- **Design Coordination**: Managed interface between architects, interior designers, and contractors
- **Budget Oversight**: Monitored construction costs and managed variations
