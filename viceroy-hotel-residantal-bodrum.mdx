## Responsibilities as QC Manager

### Quality Control Management

As QC Manager for this expansive project, I oversaw quality across all components:

- **Multi-Phase QC**: Managed quality control for three distinct project types (villas, residences, hotel) simultaneously
- **Brand Compliance**: Ensured Hilton Curio Collection standards were met for hotel component
- **Materials Approval**: Reviewed and approved materials for 76 villas, 234 residences, and 85-room hotel
- **Inspection Protocols**: Implemented systematic inspection procedures across 150,000 m² development
- **Defect Management**: Coordinated snagging and defect rectification for multiple project phases

### Construction Supervision

- **Site-Wide Coordination**: Managed QC activities across extensive site with multiple active construction zones
- **Contractor Interface**: Coordinated with multiple main contractors and dozens of sub-contractors
- **Progress Monitoring**: Tracked quality milestones across parallel construction activities
- **Technical Solutions**: Provided engineering solutions for complex construction challenges
- **Safety Oversight**: Ensured compliance with health and safety standards across large site

### Documentation & Reporting

- **Comprehensive Documentation**: Maintained detailed QC records for all project components
- **Progress Reporting**: Prepared regular quality status reports for stakeholders
- **Handover Documentation**: Compiled turnkey handover packages for villas, residences, and hotel
- **As-Built Coordination**: Managed as-built documentation for complex development
