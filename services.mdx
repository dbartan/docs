# Professional Services

With 20+ years of international experience, I provide comprehensive QA/QC management and engineering services for luxury hospitality and high-end construction projects.

---

## Core Skills & Expertise

### QA/QC Management & British Standard Compliance
- Comprehensive quality assurance and control systems aligned with international standards
- Full compliance with British Standards (BS) and other international construction standards
- Development and implementation of quality plans, procedures, and inspection protocols
- Systematic quality management systems ensuring defect-free delivery

### Client Representative Services & Investor Reporting
- Professional client representation for high-profile investors and stakeholders
- Detailed monthly progress reports and periodic status updates
- Multi-stakeholder management and communication coordination
- Budget monitoring and cost control reporting

### Turnkey Handover & Defect Management (Snagging)
- Complete turnkey project delivery management
- Comprehensive defect management and snagging procedures
- Systematic punch-list completion and quality sign-off
- FF&E (Furniture, Fixtures & Equipment) integration and delivery

### Construction Design Review & Project Management
- Constructability assessment of architectural and engineering designs
- RFI (Request for Information) coordination and design clarifications
- Shop drawing approvals and as-built documentation management
- Project schedule monitoring and critical path analysis (Primavera P6, MS Project)

### Main Contractor & Subcontractor Leadership
- Effective coordination and management of main contractors and subcontractors
- Daily supervision and direction of site activities
- Weekly progress meetings and technical coordination sessions
- Contract administration, variations, and payment approvals

### Testing & Commissioning / O&M Documentation
- Comprehensive testing and commissioning procedures for all building systems
- Material testing, quality control testing, and performance verification
- Preparation and delivery of Operation & Maintenance (O&M) documentation
- Systems integration and performance commissioning

### Materials, Mock-up & Benchmark Inspections
- Selection, approval, and quality control of premium materials
- Mock-up creation and approval processes for critical elements
- Benchmark inspections of sample rooms, bathrooms, and common areas
- Quality assurance for imported materials and specialty products

### Site Supervision & Quality Control
- Daily site inspections and supervision for all construction phases
- Quality control from foundation works to final finishes
- Verification of workmanship standards and craftsmanship quality
- Safety compliance and health & safety standards enforcement

### Luxury Brand Hospitality Standards
- Mandarin Oriental, Nikki Beach, Hilton Curio Collection brand standards
- 5-7 star hotel and boutique resort quality requirements
- Construction aligned with luxury hospitality operational standards
- Strict adherence to brand design guidelines and implementation

### International Design Team Coordination
- Collaboration with London, UK-based design offices (Purcell, Lambert & Browne)
- Coordination with international PM firms (Mace Group UK, Shaker Consultancy)
- Effective communication across multinational teams and time zones
- Cross-cultural project management and professional coordination

### High-End Fit-out Quality Assurance
- Quality assurance for luxury interior finishes and bespoke furniture
- Quality control for imported marble, custom woodwork, and designer fixtures
- FF&E procurement and installation quality management
- Inspection of bespoke architectural elements and custom design features

### Document Control & Aconex Management
- Comprehensive project documentation using Oracle Aconex
- Systematic filing, tracking, and document control systems
- Design change, variation, and impact management
- As-built documentation and turnkey delivery packages
