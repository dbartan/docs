## Responsibilities as Site Engineer

### Construction Supervision

As Site Engineer during this formative period of my career (2008-2011), I was responsible for:

- **Daily Site Supervision**: Overseeing construction activities across the development
- **Quality Control**: Ensuring construction quality and adherence to specifications
- **Technical Coordination**: Coordinating between design office and contractors
- **Materials Management**: Monitoring material deliveries and quality
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitoring construction progress against schedules
- **Problem Solving**: Addressing technical issues during construction

### Engineering Responsibilities

- **Technical Reviews**: Reviewing shop drawings and construction submittals
- **Site Measurements**: Conducting site surveys and dimensional checks
- **Concrete Supervision**: Overseeing concrete pours and structural works
- **MEP Coordination**: Coordinating mechanical, electrical, and plumbing installations
- **Foundation Works**: Supervising foundation and structural construction
- **Finishing Works**: Overseeing interior and exterior finishes

### Documentation

- **Site Reports**: Preparing daily and weekly progress reports
- **Quality Records**: Maintaining quality control documentation
- **As-Built Drawings**: Contributing to as-built documentation
- **Inspection Records**: Documenting inspections and tests
